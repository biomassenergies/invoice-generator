## Pellets and Briquettes
Eco-friendly, high-performance fuel alternatives designed for industrial and commercial use.

---

## Raw Materials and Manufacturing

### Raw Materials
- Sawdust
- Wood chips
- Rice husk
- Groundnut shells
- Bagasse

### Manufacturing Process
1. Drying (Moisture < 15%)
2. Grinding
3. High-pressure compression

Note: No additives required. Natural lignin acts as a binder.

## Products

### Biomass Briquette
- High-density eco-friendly fuel
- Made from organic waste (sawdust, groundnut shells, agro residues)
- Standard Size: 90 MM circular
- Alternative to coal (2026 industrial trend)

#### Benefits
- Carbon-neutral
- Low ash content
- Stable pricing

---

## Briquettes vs Pellets

| Feature | Pellets | Briquettes |
|--------|--------|-----------|
| Size | 6–8 mm | 90 mm |
| Application | Small-scale & automated | Industrial |
| Density | High | Moderate |
| Combustion | High energy | Long duration |
| Moisture | <10% | 15–20% |
| Production | High precision | Less intensive |
